Our Story

Glow by Bonolo is a small fictional skincare business based in Johannesburg, started in 2023 by Bonolo Motloung. The business focuses on affordable and natural skincare products mainly for young people. The idea for this brand came from the founders personal experience with acne while growing up. Dealing with acne affected her confidence and how je saw herself. Because of this, she wanted to create products that help others feel more comfortable in and confident in their own skin. The business currently works on social media, where it connects with customers and promotes products. So glow by Bonolo is a skincare brand that focuses on enhancing and emnbracing natural beauty.
